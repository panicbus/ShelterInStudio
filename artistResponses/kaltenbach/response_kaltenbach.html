Stephen Kaltenbach

How has being stuck at home affected your art practice?
I have been cleaning, painting and organizing my studio. I have continued work on the painting of Christ in Glory. I have taken up work on the Chrystal Ought, a 5' x 7' painting paused in 1983.
Have you created new work during this period?
No. My work generally takes years.
Has your work changed in any way during this period?
No.
What have some of your challenges been?
They remain the same: getting my old body out into my work space, trying to solve the mystery of how the work should look and how to achieve that.
What have some of your successes been?
There has been an increase in enthusiasm as some of the work has passed through the awkward stage and entered the promising period.
